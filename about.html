Who We Are

The Odyssey Sourcing are the trusted garment buying house based in Bangladesh, specializing in sourcing and supplying high-quality apparel for global brands and retailers. With strong relationships across leading factories, we ensure every stage of production is managed with precision — from design development and raw material sourcing to manufacturing oversight and final shipment.

With over a decade of dedicated service in the apparel export industry, we have consistently delivered high-quality garments with integrity and commitment to a diverse clientele across the United Kingdom, Europe, the United States, Canada, Africa, and Asia.

With a deep understanding of fashion trends, strong vendor networks, and strict quality control practices, we ensure that every product meets the highest standards of quality, compliance, and timely delivery. Our professional team is committed to maintaining transparent communication, ethical sourcing, and sustainable production practices.

At The Odyssey Sourcing, we prioritize long-term and sustainable business relationships. Our guiding principle is to deliver value to our clients by offering competitive pricing, uncompromised quality, and on-time delivery. Our strength lies in our professional approach, product reliability, and dedication to customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To be a trusted and dynamic garment buying house based in Bangladesh, delivering high-quality apparel solutions to global clients. We strive to:

  • Build long-term relationships by offering reliable, ethical, and value-driven sourcing services.
  • Ensure premium quality, competitive pricing, and timely delivery across all product categories.
  • Promote transparency, sustainability, and compliance in every step of our supply chain.
  • Foster a safe, clean, and inclusive working environment for our employees and partners.
  • Continuously innovate and evolve to meet the changing demands of the international fashion industry.

We are dedicated to turning our clients’ concepts into reality—seamlessly, ethically, and efficiently.

Vision

At Odyssey Sourcing, our vision is to emerge as a globally trusted garment buying house from Bangladesh—recognized for our unwavering commitment to quality, ethical sourcing, and customer satisfaction.

We aspire to lead with integrity and innovation, setting new standards in apparel sourcing by:

  • Building long-term partnerships based on transparency and trust.
  • Promoting sustainable and socially responsible practices.
  • Delivering value-driven solutions tailored to each client’s unique needs.
  • Representing the excellence of Bangladesh’s RMG sector on the global stage.

Through continuous improvement and a passion for perfection, we envision becoming the preferred sourcing partner for fashion brands worldwide.
